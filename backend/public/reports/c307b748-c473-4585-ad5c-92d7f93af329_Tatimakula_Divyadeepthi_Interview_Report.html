📚 What a Good Answer Looks Like
Streams are objects that let you read data from a source or write data to a destination in a continuous fashion — without loading the entire content into memory. Four types: Readable (e.g., fs.createReadStream()), Writable (e.g., fs.createWriteStream()), Duplex (both), and Transform (modify data as it passes through).
